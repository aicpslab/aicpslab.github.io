PhD Student Openings

  • I am looking for highly self-motivated students with interests in one or more of the following areas to join my group: control systems, machine learning, formal methods, and cyber-physical systems. Full financial support is expected to be in the form of research/teaching assistantships to cover the tuition and living expenses.

  • Ideal candidates should meet the following qualifications:

    • B.S. or M.S. in Computer Science/Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Automation, Mathematics, or related fields. M.S. with project experience and publication record is preferred.

    • Strong background in control theory, machine learning, optimization, formal methods is preferred.

    • Good programming skills in Python, or Matlab.

    • Additional PhD admission criteria at Augusta University are referred to PhD program page.

  • The positions will remain open until filled by qualified candidates. If you are interested, please email your CV to Prof. Xiang at wxiang@augusta.edu.
